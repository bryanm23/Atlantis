FAQ

Quick answers to the questions creators usually ask on the first call.

Do I really stay hands-off?

Yes—completely hands-off. We handle content creation, posting schedules, engagement, DMs, and conversion. You can review and approve if you want, or we can run it entirely without your input. Your choice.

How does the performance-based pricing work?

We only make money when you do. Our fee is tied to your revenue growth—typically a percentage of the revenue we help generate. If you don't make money, we don't make money. It's that simple.

What platforms do you manage?

TikTok, Instagram (Reels, Stories, Posts), X/Twitter, Threads, and more. We handle cross-platform content creation, posting, and engagement so you don't have to think about it.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. We never disclose that you're working with us, and we don't use your name or brand in any marketing materials. Your partnership with Atlantis Talent Group stays completely private—we operate behind the scenes.

How fast can we start?

Typically within 7–14 days depending on onboarding and access. We'll audit your accounts, understand your goals, and take over management quickly.

Ready to grow?

Tell us your goals and current revenue. For now, reach out via your preferred channel and we'll walk you through how we take over your social media completely hands-off, with pricing that only works if you make more money.
